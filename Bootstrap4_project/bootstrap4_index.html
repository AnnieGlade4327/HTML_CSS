The Academy Cinemas

Enjoy the Show!

Accomodations

Located in the beautiful Ashley National Forest of what is known as "The Armpit of Utah", our reels are hoping to get spinning before we lose our minds from boredom out here!

Run by homegrown Utahn's with a love for all things Cin-eh-ma, our goal is to show guests a great time, while featuring our favorite classics, local artists and aspiring filmmakers!

Our auditorium is equipped with a full beer and coffee bar, fresh juice, and we offer the only IMAX screen in the Basin!

Relax in our extra-wide cushioned seats, enjoy some farm-fresh food, and let our Cinema-Shuttle get you back home safely!
*we offer rides/shuttles to and from showtimes within the Duchesne, Fruitland, Tabiona, Hannah, Boneta and Altamont regions*
