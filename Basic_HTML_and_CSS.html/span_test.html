Activites

Activites can depend on the breed of the dog
but it is always a good idea to get a harness to help with leash training
if you have a puppy or a dog that has not been off-leash trained.

Scheduled Walks

Consisteny is very important for dogs, whether they are puppies are just adjusting to a new home.
If someone decides to get a dog, they should make sure they are alloting time in their schedule for regular walks throughout the day,
with some extra activities and mental stimulation such as hiking, jogging, swimming, etc.
